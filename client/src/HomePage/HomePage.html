How does it works

Follow the next few steps and get started..
Create a manager
account

Once a manager account is established, you can immediately add stores and shops, as well as assign employees to each location. Subsequently, you will have access to all employee information and can navigate to either the shop or store dashboard to view daily and monthly profit summaries, total shop balances, and receive notifications regarding activities within each shop.

Employee
login

As a manager, you can assign a password and email address to each of your employees, enabling them to log in and directly access their dashboard. From there, they can perform various tasks such as adding bills, creating bonds, managing employee records, viewing all bills, adding subjects, and editing existing subjects, among other responsibilities.

Start
working

Each employee receives specific permissions from the manager, resulting in customized interfaces tailored to their individual roles. This ensures that each employee focuses on their designated tasks, effectively dividing the workload and enhancing overall security.
